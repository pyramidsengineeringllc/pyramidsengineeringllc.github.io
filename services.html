Professional engineering services.

Pyramids provides a wide variety of professional engineering services that include the following:

  • Conceptual civil site plans development, preparation, and review.
  • Rough civil grading plans development, preparation, and review.
  • Precise civil grading plans development, preparation, and review.
  • Drainage studies and design & drawings/report preparation, and review.
  • Storm water management (quantity and quality) design & drawings/report preparation, and review.
  • Culvert and storm drain system design & hydraulic modeling performance.
  • Street design and plans & profiles preparation and review.
  • Final Site plans preparation and review.
  • Demolition plans preparation.
  • Sediment and erosion control design & drawings preparation.
  • Storm Water Pollution Prevention Plan (SWPPP) preparation.
  • On-Site water utility design & drawings/report preparation.
  • On-Site Sanitary Sewer utility design & drawings/report preparation.
  • Engineering technical Specifications development and review.
  • Construction probable cost estimate preparation.
  • Estimated Construction Schedule preparation.
  • Construction/design bid package preparation and review.
  • Plans check services performance.
  • Management & administration services performance.
  • Easement, lot line adjustment, and lots merging documents preparation and review.
  • Permits obtaining and expediting.
  • Grants obtaining and securing.
  • Traffic studies and traffic control plans preparation and review.
  • Feasibility studies preparation and review.
  • Civil/structural/geotechnical retaining wall design & drawings/report preparation and review.
  • Structural design development engineering services (calculations, reports, and drawings).
  • Bidding assistance services performance.
  • Construction administration services that include constructability reviews, shop drawings/materials/data/cut-sheets review and approval, resolving design issues, responding to requests for information (RFI), and requests for clarifications (RFCs), minor design modification to accommodate unforeseen conditions, site inspection to ensure compliance with the bid documents, managing/attending construction meetings & issuing meeting minutes, reviewing, negotiating, and approving of change orders.
  • CIP Street/roadway reconstruction and pavement rehabilitation design & drawings/reports preparation and review.
  • CIP water/sewer/storm drain utility pipelines (small & large diameters) design, hydraulic modeling, and drawings/reports preparation and review.
  • CIP Pumping stations, PRVs, storage, and well facility infrastructure planning, technical engineering design, and plans preparation and review.
  • Water system structural components design including special thrust Blocks, special vaults, and platforms preparation and review.
  • Construction As-Builts and final records preparation.
  • Regulatory compliance services performance.
